Your Central Hub for VEIN Modding

This wiki is a community-driven repository of knowledge for modding VEIN. Our goal is to provide clear, concise, and up-to-date documentation for every aspect of the modding process. Whether you're a beginner looking to change a simple texture or an expert aiming to create complex gameplay mechanics, you'll find the resources you need right here.

We cover everything from the foundational tools and concepts to advanced techniques in blueprinting and memory editing. Explore our tutorials, learn best practices, and join our Discord community to connect with fellow modders.
