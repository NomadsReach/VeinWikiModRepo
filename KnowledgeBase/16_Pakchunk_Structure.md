## Pakchunk Organization

Pakchunks are organized by:
- Core content (pakchunk0-2)
- DLC content (pakchunk3+)
- Marketplace assets
- Engine content

## Modding Implications

When creating mods:
- Use high pakchunk numbers (100+) to avoid conflicts
- Keep related assets in same pakchunk
- Consider load order dependencies
- Test pakchunk priority
