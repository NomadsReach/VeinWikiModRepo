## Vehicle Systems

Vehicles in VEIN typically feature:
- Fuel consumption
- Damage and durability
- Storage capacity
- Speed and handling
- Required parts for operation

## Modding Guide

### Creating Custom Vehicles

1. Copy existing vehicle template
2. Modify properties:
   - Speed and acceleration
   - Fuel capacity
   - Storage space
   - Durability
3. Set required parts
4. Configure sounds and effects
5. Test physics and handling

### Vehicle Balance

- Fuel should be scarce resource
- Parts should require maintenance
- Storage should be valuable
- Speed should come with risks
- Noise should attract attention
