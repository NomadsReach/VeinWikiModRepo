Important Note for Mod Uploaders

This is serious! We do not want to burden the game developers with extra work!

If you upload your mods ANYWHERE, you must include this disclaimer:

***IMPORTANT NOTE!!!***

If you experience ANY bugs or glitches while you have this mod, or any others, installed, please make sure to remove the mods and test if the bug continues before reporting it to the developers!!!
